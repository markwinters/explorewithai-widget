Final Example

This page loads the widget from the local dist/explore-ai.min.js file. The floating button should appear in the bottom-right corner.

How it works

Click the ✨ Explore With AI button to open the provider menu. Select an AI platform to open this page's content in that tool.

What's collected

Page URL, title, and meta description — nothing else. No analytics, no tracking, no backend.

Browser support

Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox (last 2 major versions). Framework agnostic.

Light / Dark

Auto-detects your system preference. Force with data-theme="dark" on the script tag.
