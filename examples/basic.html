Welcome to My Website

This example page demonstrates the Explore With AI widget. Click the button in the bottom-right corner to try it.

How It Works

The widget collects the current page URL, title, and meta description, then sends this data to your chosen AI provider when you click an option in the menu.

Features

🔗 Universal Bridge

Works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot. Your page data is sent directly to the provider you select.

🎨 Zero Configuration

Add one script tag and you're done. The widget automatically detects your page content and renders a polished floating button.

🌓 Theme Aware

Automatically matches your system's light/dark preference. Can also be forced via data-theme attribute.
